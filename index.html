Hom'age

1.0.0.0.0

The galatic civilization of Hom'age has thrived over the previous millenia. The Aegis Assembly has overseen the expansion of the galaxy since its inception after The Old War. Hom'age is split into three habitable "arms" (Haven, Hearth, and Holm) that stretch out from the galatic core. Between each arm are starless strans of dead space that have impeaded any attempt to cross over into the other arms. With great loss of life, a pathway was found connecting the Haven and Hearth arms. Still, no such path has been found to the Holm arm.

Tellus

0.1.0.0.0

Part of a binary star system, Tellus' temperature rises to fifty degrees centigrade. It's surface is covered by vast deserts, and has seen countless planetary events that have decimated many civilizations in its history. The inhabitants of the world have named their twin stars Rei and Sel, and naming their two major societies after them.

Gaea

0.2.0.0.0

A vibrant planet with three sister planets orbiting around it and the Tower of the Heavens. Godess Aelia watched over her children with love and compassion. Bestowing her offspring with the gift of control over the elements, they turned on one another and waged a deadly war. When Aelia saw the horrors that her children committed with her gift, she ripped it away from them and cast out all of the races, save two from Gaea. She sundered the land and lifted her citidel from the ground to sit high above in the skies creating a devastaing and continual maelstrom at the center of the planet. The remaining inhabitants on Gaea reside on three continents that surround the floating Tower of the Heavens.

Terra

0.3.0.0.0

A small satelite orbiting a gas giant. The inhabitants of Terra once worshipped the Beginners. Three beings of incredible power that carved out the mountains and valleys from the prehistoric visage of rock. They brought life to Terra and cultivated the lush landscapes. They sealed themselves away deep within the planet once their work completed. Now the children of the Beginners, the Nairu are left to live their lives.
