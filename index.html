Hom'age

1.0.0.0.0

The galatic civilization of Hom'age has thrived over the previous millenia. The Aegis Assembly has overseen the expansion of the galaxy since its inception after The Old War. Hom'age is split into three habitable "arms" (Haven, Hearth, and Holm) that stretch out from the galatic core. Between each arm are starless strans of dead space that have impeaded any attempt to cross over into the other arms. With great loss of life, a pathway was found connecting the Haven and Hearth arms. Still, no such path has been found to the Holm arm.
